# Manage Labels

### Managing labels

After you have added labels you can edit or delete the labels, whenever required.

![](https://developer.imagimob.com/Images/labels.png)

#### Editing labels

You can edit labels by resizing the labels, moving the label or changing the label name.

##### Resizing the label

To resize the label, follow the steps:

1. - Select the label you want to resize.
2. - Click and drag the sizing handles that appear on the sides of the label to the desired position.
  OR
  Right-click on the label and select Extend label left or Extend label right to extend the label to extreme length or right respectively.

##### Moving the label

To move the label, follow the steps:

1. - Select the label you want to re-position.
2. - Click and drag the label to the desired position.
  OR
  Right-click on the label to **Cut** and **Paste** the label to the desired position.

##### Changing the label name

To change the label name, follow the steps:

1. - Select the label you want to rename.
2. - Right-click on the label and select **Label properties** from the list of options.The label properties window appears.
3. - In **Label** enter the new name of the label.
4. - Click **Close**.

#### Deleting labels

To delete the label, follow the steps:

1. - Select the label you want to delete.
2. - Right-click and select **Delete** from the list of options. A label is deleted from the track.
