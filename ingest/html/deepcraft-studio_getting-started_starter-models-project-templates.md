### Studio Accelerators and Project Templates

We provide DEEPCRAFT™ Studio Accelerators and Project Templates to facilitate your Edge AI journey with DEEPCRAFT™ Studio. We offer a wide range of Studio Accelerators that include the datasets, preprocessing, model architecture and instructions that developers need to turn them into production-ready models. Studio Accelerators cover a wide variety of use cases and sensors, and are ready to be refined and trained based on your needs. To know more, refer to [DEEPCRAFT™ Studio Accelerators](https://developer.imagimob.com/getting-started/starter-models-project-templates/starter-models). Project templates allows you to create a machine learning model from scratch. To know more, refer to [Project Templates](https://developer.imagimob.com/getting-started/starter-models-project-templates/project-template).
