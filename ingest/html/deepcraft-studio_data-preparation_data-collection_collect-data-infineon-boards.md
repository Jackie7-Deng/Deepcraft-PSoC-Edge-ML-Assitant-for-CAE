### Real-time data collection using Infineon kits

This topic provides information on how to stream real-data from various sensors present in the **PSOC™ Edge E84 AI Kit**, **PSOC Edge E84 Evaluation Kit** and **PSOC™ 6 AI Evaluation Kit** into DEEPCRAFT™ Studio. You can add additional data to your existing projects or build your own dataset from scratch. You can collect both labeled and unlabeled data, depending on your project requirements.

Before collecting data, flash the kits with the streaming firmware. For step-by-step instructions, refer to the following topics:

- - [Streaming Firmware for PSOC™ Edge E84 AI Kit](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-studio/getting-started/infineon-boards/psoc-edge-e84-ai-kit#streaming-firmware-for-psoc-edge-e84-ai-kit)
- - [Streaming Firmware for PSOC™ Edge E84 Evaluation Kit](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-studio/getting-started/infineon-boards/psoc-edge-e84-eval-kit#streaming-firmware-for-psoc-edge-e84-evaluation-kit)
- - [Streaming Firmware for PSOC™ 6 AI Evaluation Kit](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-studio/getting-started/infineon-boards/infineon-ai-evaluation-kit#streaming-firmware-for-psoc-6-ai-evaluation-kit)

After flashing the Streaming Firmware, refer to the following topics to know how to collect data in detail.

- - [Real-Time Data Streaming with PSOC™ Edge E84 Artificial Intelligence Kit](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-studio/data-preparation/data-collection/collect-data-infineon-boards/collect-data-edge-ai-kit): Stream data from microphone, IMU, magnetometer, Pressure and Temperature, Humidity and Temperature and radar in real-time.
- - [Real-Time Data Streaming with PSOC™ Edge E84 Evaluation Kit](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-studio/data-preparation/data-collection/collect-data-infineon-boards/collect-data-edge-evaluation-kit): Stream data from microphone, IMU and magnetometer in real-time.
- - [Real-Time Data Streaming with PSOC™ 6 AI Evaluation Kit](https://developer.imagimob.com/data-preparation/data-collection/collect-data-infineon-boards/data-collection-ai-kit): Stream data from microphone, IMU, magnetometer, Pressure, and radar in real-time.
