### DEEPCRAFT™ Ready Models

DEEPCRAFT™ Ready Models (formerly Imagimob Ready Models) are production-ready AI / Machine Learning (ML) models that are ready to be added to any edge device. You save all the time, cost and resources required to create collect, validate, and label data, as well as to train custom AI / ML models on that data. We offer a wide range of DEEPCRAFT™ Ready Models developed to meet various business scenarios. Refer to our [official website](https://www.imagimob.com/ready-models) to know more.

### How to obtain the DEEPCRAFT™ Ready Models?

1. - Navigate to our [official website for Ready Models](https://www.imagimob.com/ready-models).
2. - Click the **Test Now** button of the model you want to download. Fill out the form and click submit. You will receive an email with a link to download the necessary files and additional information.
3. - Open the email and click **Download Your Model** link to access the files.

After you download the required Ready Model refer to the following sections for detailed instructions for testing the Ready Model:

- - [Testing using pre-compiled binary/hex files](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-ready-models/testing-using-precompiled-binary-hex-files)
- - [Testing using static library in a custom applications](https://developer.imagimob.com/deepcraft-ready-models/testing-using-static-library-custom-application)

### Choose Your Ready Model

We offer the following Ready Models:

#### Audio Ready Models

1. - **Baby Cry Detection** - The Baby Cry model is designed to be used in smart home or lifestyle products that want to alert adults to the sound of baby or toddler crying in another room, or to activate baby soothing features. The model can capture most of the baby cry sounds at up to 5 meters distance from device and suitable for 0-4 years old. The model is robust against the most common background sounds, particularly indoor. Refer to the [Baby Cry Detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Baby%20Cry%20Detection%20test%20report.pdf) for more information.
2. - **Cough Detection** - The Coughing model is designed for use in healthcare products or wearables. It detects coughing, which can indicate illness or other respiratory conditions, and can be used to identify potential environmental hazards.
  The model can capture at least 94% of coughs and measures coughs per hour to identify ‘sick’ versus ‘healthy’ user. The model is robust against the most common indoor and outdoor background sounds. Refer to the [Cough Detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Cough%20Detection%20test%20report.pdf) for more information.
3. - **Direction of Arrival** - The Direction of Arrival model is designed to determine the direction from which the sounds are coming from. The uses for this model are many and varied, for example, paired with the Siren Ready Model, it can add safety for drivers, passengers and pedestrians. Refer to the [Direction of Arrival Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Direction%20of%20Arrival%20(audio)%20test%20report.pdf) for more information.
4. - **Factory Alarm detection** - The Factory Alarms and Sirens model is designed to be used in factory settings to detect alarms and sirens when they go off. This makes factories safer, and can be used for example to alert workers wearing noise cancelling headphones that a siren or alarm has gone off, which they might not otherwise hear it. The model is robust against diverse factory background sounds, and different distances and angles from the sound source. Refer to the [Factory Alarm detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Factory%20Alarm%20Detection%20test%20report.pdf) for more information
5. - **Siren Detection** - The Siren detection model uses audio event detection to identify emergency vehicle sirens. This can be used to alert pedestrians to emergency vehicles in their vicinity, or to trigger other safety features in wearables.
  The model can capture over 95% of siren sounds coming from all directions. The model is robust against common traffic background sounds in different environments. Refer to the [Siren Detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Siren%20Detection%20test%20report.pdf) for more information
6. - **Snore Detection** - The Snore detection model is ideal for companies with healthcare or wearable products that want to identify snoring. This important feature can be used to identify health or environmental issues. The model can capture at least 96% of snores and flexibly designed to be used in wearables or in devices that are placed near the bed. The model is robust against the most common background sounds, particularly indoor. Refer to the [Snore Detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Snore%20Detection%20test%20report.pdf) for more information

Watch the YouTube videos to learn how to quickly deploy and evaluate the DEEPCRAFT™ Ready Model for [**Baby Cry Detection**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hlbzafZTiyc), [**Cough Detection**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WOM4A7eSshM), and [**Siren Detection**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zb3OxRsrm64) on a test kit.

#### Gesture Detection Ready Model

The Gesture Detection model is created to make smart home or lifestyle products easier to use. Wouldn’t it be convenient to add time to your microwave by just swiping left or swiping up to turn on your coffee machine. This radar-based model recognizes five hand gestures. The use cases for your products are nearly limitless. The model is robust against many variations, such as sitting or standing, or different user heights. Refer to the [Gesture Detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Gesture%20Recognition%20test%20report.pdf) for more information.

Watch the YouTube video to learn how to quickly deploy and evaluate the [**Gesture Detection Ready Model**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D_A99_z4lX0) on a test kit.

#### Fall Detection Ready Model

The Fall Detection model is perfect to add into wrist worn devices. It is specifically developed to understand motion and recognize falling, and at the same time reduce battery consumption to a minimum, so that the wearable maintains a long battery life. This model provides safety features for all users, but is particularly useful for seniors. Refer to the [Fall Detection Ready Model Report](https://email.imagimob.com/hubfs/Test%20documents%20for%20Ready%20Models/DEEPCRAFT%20Ready%20Model%20for%20Fall%20Detection%20test%20report.pdf) for more information.

Watch the YouTube video to learn how to quickly deploy and evaluate the [**Fall Detection Ready Model**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WRgD8FWPiLs) on a test kit.
